# ShopEase Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024  
**Version:** 2.1  
**Last Updated:** November 15, 2024

---

## 1. General Return Window

| Customer Tier | Return Window |
|---------------|---------------|
| Standard      | 30 days from delivery date |
| Gold          | 45 days from delivery date |
| Platinum      | 60 days from delivery date |

Returns requested after the applicable window will be **automatically denied**.

---

## 2. Category-Specific Rules

### 2.1 Electronics
- Maximum return window: **14 days** from delivery date (overrides tier-based window).
- Item must be in original packaging with all accessories included.
- Item must show no signs of use, damage, or tampering.
- Software licenses bundled with hardware follow digital product rules.

### 2.2 Clothing & Apparel
- Standard tier window applies (30/45/60 days based on tier).
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached.
- Undergarments and swimwear are **non-returnable** for hygiene reasons.

### 2.3 Home & Kitchen
- Standard tier window applies.
- Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Opened consumables (candles, food items) are non-returnable.

### 2.4 Digital Products
- **All digital products are non-refundable** once delivered/accessed.
- This includes: software licenses, online courses, e-books, and digital downloads.
- No exceptions unless required by local law.

---

## 3. Final Sale Items

- Items marked as **"Final Sale"** are **non-refundable** under any circumstances.
- Final sale status is displayed at checkout and on the order confirmation.
- Damaged final sale items may qualify for exchange only (not refund).

---

## 4. Refund Limits & Abuse Prevention

- Maximum **3 refunds per customer per calendar year**.
- Customers who have reached this limit will have all refund requests **denied** until the next calendar year.
- Refund amount cannot exceed the original order total for the specified item(s).
- Partial refunds are allowed only for individual items within a multi-item order.

---

## 5. Order Status Requirements

- Refunds are only processed for orders with status **"delivered"**.
- Orders that are "shipped", "processing", or "pending" are not eligible.
- If an order has not been delivered, the customer should request cancellation instead.

---

## 6. Refund Processing

### Approved Refunds
- Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
- A confirmation email is sent upon approval.
- Refund reference number format: `REF-YYYY-XXXXX`

### Denied Refunds
- Customer receives a clear explanation citing the specific policy rule violated.
- Denial reference number format: `DEN-YYYY-XXXXX`

---

## 7. Required Information for Refund Requests

The agent must verify ALL of the following before processing:

1. **Customer identity** — email or order ID must match CRM records
2. **Order ID** — must exist in the customer's order history
3. **Item SKU** — must be present in the specified order
4. **Reason for return** — must be documented
5. **Policy compliance** — all applicable rules must be checked via tools

---

## 8. Escalation Rules

- Refund requests over **$500** require noting "manager review recommended" but can be auto-processed if all policy checks pass.
- Disputed denials should be logged for human review.
- VIP/Platinum customers with 0 refunds this year and orders under $200 may receive expedited processing.

---

## 9. Prohibited Actions

- Never approve refunds for digital products.
- Never approve refunds for final sale items.
- Never exceed the customer's annual refund limit.
- Never approve refunds for undelivered orders.
- Never approve refunds outside the applicable return window.
